title: "Teams"
description: "Team registration, membership, and coaching"
---

A **team** is the primary competing unit in the FLL system. Teams are identified by name (their natural key), belong to a city, and compete in a category. They carry roster, coaching, and edition-registration relationships.

## Team fields

| Field | Type | Constraints |
|---|---|---|
| `name` | `String` | Primary key; 3–50 characters, required |
| `city` | `String` | Max 100 characters, required |
| `foundationYear` | `Integer` | Must be ≥ 1998, required |
| `educationalCenter` | `String` | Max 100 characters, optional |
| `category` | `String` | Required, non-blank |
| `inscriptionDate` | `LocalDate` | Past or present; auto-set to today if not provided |

<Note>
The team `name` is the primary key. It must be unique across the system and between 3 and 50 characters long.
</Note>

## Team members

A team can have up to **10 members**. Each member is its own entity linked back to the team.

| Field | Type | Constraints |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | `Long` | Auto-generated |
| `name` | `String` | 2–100 characters, required |
| `birthDate` | `LocalDate` | Must be in the past, required |
| `gender` | `String` | Max 20 characters, optional |
| `tShirtSize` | `String` | Max 10 characters, optional |
| `role` | `String` | Max 50 characters, required |
| `team` | `Team` | Owning team (required) |

Members are created by the `TeamMember.create(name, role, birthDate, team)` factory, which automatically calls `team.addMember(member)`. Attempting to add an 11th member throws an error.

## Coaches

A coach is an independent entity with a unique email address who can be assigned to one or more teams.

| Field | Type | Constraints |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | `Integer` | Auto-generated |
| `name` | `String` | Required, non-blank |
| `emailAddress` | `String` | Valid email, unique across all coaches |
| `phoneNumber` | `String` | Optional |

### Coach assignment rules

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Maximum coaches per team">
    A team can have at most **2 coaches**. Calling `team.addCoach(coach)` when 2 are already assigned raises `MAX_COACHES_PER_TEAM_REACHED`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Maximum teams per coach">
    A coach can train at most **2 teams**. If the coach already trains 2 teams, the call raises `MAX_TEAMS_PER_COACH_REACHED`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Duplicate assignment">
    Assigning the same coach to a team a second time raises `COACH_ALREADY_ASSIGNED`.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Floaters

A team can be supported by up to **2 floaters** (a type of volunteer — see [Volunteers](/concepts/volunteers)). Floaters can assist multiple teams.

- Adding a third floater to a team raises `"A team cannot have more than 2 floaters"`.
- Floaters are removed cleanly via `team.removeFloater(floater)`, which keeps both sides of the relationship in sync.

## Registering to an edition

To participate in an edition, a team must be registered to it. Registration is only allowed when the edition is in the `OPEN` state (see [Editions](/concepts/editions)).

The many-to-many relationship is maintained in the `edition_teams` join table. A team can be registered to multiple editions over its lifetime.

```http
POST /editions/{editionId}/teams
{ "teamName": "RoboTeam" }
```

<Warning>
An edition accepts a maximum of **18 teams**. Registering when the cap has been reached returns a `409 Conflict` with the error code `MAX_TEAMS_REACHED`.
</Warning>

## Validation summary

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| `name` length | 3–50 characters |
| `city` length | 1–100 characters |
| `foundationYear` | ≥ 1998 |
| `inscriptionDate` | Past or present only |
| Members per team | Maximum 10 |
| Coaches per team | Maximum 2 |
| Teams per coach | Maximum 2 |
| Floaters per team | Maximum 2 |
| Teams per edition | Maximum 18 |
